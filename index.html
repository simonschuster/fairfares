DoD per diem rates — this destination

Rate component Amount (USD) How it works
Maximum lodging—Nightly cap for hotel. You are reimbursed actual cost up to this limit.
Local meals (M&IE)—Full daily meals allowance. International = per diem — claim as flat rate, no receipts needed.
Proportional meals—Used on travel days (first/last day). Approx. 75% of full M&IE rate.
Local incidental—Tips, small fees. Included within the M&IE total above.
Maximum per diem—Lodging + M&IE combined daily maximum.
Actuals vs per diemFor international travel, meals can be claimed as a flat per diem — no receipts needed up to the daily rate. Lodging is claimed as actuals (what you actually paid) up to the maximum lodging rate. Domestic travel is different: meals are actuals only up to $92/day, never a flat per diem.
