How it works

Three steps to a defensible answer

No API keys, no subscriptions to start. Just enter the route and get a benchmark range backed by real data sources.

1

Enter the trip details

Origin, destination, travel date, cabin class, and trip type. City names or IATA codes both work.

2

Get the benchmark range

FairFares returns a low, typical, and high estimate drawn from BTS historical data and curated market rates.

3

Enter the submitted amount

FairFares gives an instant verdict — within benchmark, borderline, or over — and generates a shareable PDF record.
