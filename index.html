Maximum lodging—Nightly hotel cap. Claimed as actuals up to this limit.
Local meals (M&IE)—Full daily meals allowance. International = per diem — no receipts needed up to this rate.
Proportional meals—For travel days (first/last day). ~75% of full M&IE rate.

Maximum per diem—Lodging + M&IE combined daily maximum.
