Under UCOP G-28, travelers should use the most economical ground transport. If a traveler rented a car when a taxi or rideshare would have sufficed, reimbursement is limited to what the taxi would have cost along the same route.

TaxiFareFinder uses actual metered rates for each city. Screenshot the result and attach to the expense record as your comp.

International and OCONUS travel (including Hawaii and Alaska) uses DoD per diem rates under UCOP G-28. Look up the rate for any country and city below.

DoD per diem covers lodging, meals and incidental expenses (M&IE). Under G-28, international hotel reimbursement is limited to 300% of the DoD lodging rate.
